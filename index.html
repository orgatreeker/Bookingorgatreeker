None at all. A complete video tutorial walks through every tab. Most people are set up within 20 minutes. All calculations happen automatically — you just enter your numbers.

Free is one tab — a basic monthly tracker. Pro is a complete system with 9+ tabs, automated dashboards, investment tracker, EMI planner, and annual dashboard.
Is it built for Indian expenses?▾
Yes — designed for Indian financial life. EMIs, SIPs, UPI habits, family transfers, festival season. Customise currency, categories, and expense types.
Does it work on mobile?▾
Yes. Built on Google Sheets — works on Android and iOS. Enter transactions on phone, review dashboards on desktop.
